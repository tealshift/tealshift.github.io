This chart shows per-game average stats for various Rocket League players. Most of the listed players are professionals.

Rocket League is a popular soccer video game played with rocket-powered cars. In the game you can:

- score a point with a goal

- get an assist by passing to a teammate who then scores a goal

- get a save by blocking an opponent's shot on your goal

- get a demolition (inflict demo) by ramming an opponent at full speed which temporarily removes them from the field

- get yourself demolished (suffer demo) by getting demoed by an opponent
