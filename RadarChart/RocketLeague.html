Rocket League Player Comparison





This chart shows per-game average stats for various Rocket League players. Select players from the dropdown menus for comparison. Hover over the graphs for the numbers.

Rocket League is a popular soccer video game played with rocket-powered cars. Achievements in the game include:

  • Goal: Scoring the ball in opponent's net
  • Assist: Passing to a teammate who then scores a goal
  • Save: Blocking an opponent's shot on your goal
  • Inflict Demo: Demolition by ramming an opponent at full speed which temporarily removes them from the field
  • Suffer Demo: Getting yourself demolished by an opponent
